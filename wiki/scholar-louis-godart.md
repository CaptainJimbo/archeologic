Belgian-Italian epigrapher and Aegean-scripts scholar, a leading authority on the Phaistos Disk and on Linear A/B from [[site-minoan-crete]]. His 1995 study of the disk (cited in [[source-reczko-2009]]) dates it broadly within the Middle/Late Minoan range and treats it, with the rest of mainstream Aegean scholarship, as a genuine Bronze Age artifact whose script is simply undeciphered.

Included as a representative of the consensus that underpins [[claim-phaistos-disc-authentic]]: the specialists who have worked most closely on the object read it as authentic. His dating attribution is grounded via the Reczko fetch; his monograph itself was not fetched this run, so no separate source node is created for it.
