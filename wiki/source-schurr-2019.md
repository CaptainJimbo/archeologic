Peer-reviewed article (Gephyra, open access); full text fetched. Argues the widely accepted link between Homer's Troy and the Hittite land of Wilusa/Taruwisa rests on 'little other than name associations' (Alaksandu~Alexandros, Wilusa~Ilios, appaliunas~Apollo), none cogent, and that attempts to prove a town around the Hisarlik stronghold have failed — 'there is no connection with Hittite history or culture.' Directly attacks the Hittite-texts plank of the historicity case.
