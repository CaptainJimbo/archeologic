Anatomist at the University of Bristol and, with John Prag and the medical
artist Richard Neave, part of the team that produced the famous facial
reconstruction of the Tomb II skull and defended the Philip II identification.
Lead author of [[source-musgrave-2010]].

Grouped here as one node with the [[scholar-john-prag|Prag]]–Neave collaboration
because they argue as a unit. They are the standard-bearers for
[[claim-tomb-ii-philip-ii]] and the [[claim-tomb-ii-green-cremation|green
cremation]] reading — and, having built the original reconstruction, they are
defending their own earlier conclusion, which the graph records rather than
hides.
