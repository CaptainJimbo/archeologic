Celticist and philologist (University of Navarra) who defends a historical Arthur against the mythical-figure school. In [[source-breeze-2016]] he argues Arthur really existed as a North Briton (perhaps a Strathclyder) whose battles lay in southern Scotland and the Borders, dating Camlann to 537 after the Welsh annals. He represents the minority historicist position and explicitly aligns with the older Northern-Arthur proponents (Rachel Bromwich, Charles Thomas) against skeptics.
