Peer-reviewed note (BSA, 1997); abstract + references fetched from Cambridge Core. Observes that the Hittite tablet KUB XXIII.13 reports Hittite warfare in western/NW Asia Minor c. 1240 BC (the Seha River Land against Tudhaliya IV), *contemporary* with the destruction of Mycenaean [[claim-troy-vi-viia-war-destruction|Troy VI]] — raising the possibility that this conflict provided historical background for the *Iliad*. Explicitly a cautious 'possibility', not proof. The strongest documentary plank on the pro-kernel side.
