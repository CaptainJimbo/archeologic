Archaeogeneticist (Max Planck Institute for the Science of Human History, Jena; University of Tuebingen). Lead author of the 2000-year Etruscan archaeogenomic time transect ([[source-posth-2021]], senior author Johannes Krause), the study whose finding of no recent Anatolian admixture is the central genetic evidence disputing [[claim-etruscan-anatolian-origin]]. His work also frames the still-open puzzle recorded in [[claim-etruscan-language-origin]].
