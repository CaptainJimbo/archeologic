Historian of Anglo-Saxon and post-Roman Britain (University of Manchester), author of King Arthur: The Making of the Legend (Yale, 2018) among earlier works on the sub-Roman period. He argues Arthur is best understood as a literary and legendary construct rather than a documented 5th-6th century figure, and traces how the legend was built from late sources.
