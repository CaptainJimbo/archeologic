American antiquities dealer, editor of Minerva magazine and a specialist in detecting art forgeries. In 2008 he published the principal modern challenge to the disk's authenticity — an article arguing the Phaistos Disk is a hoax fabricated in or around 1908, the year Luigi Pernier (see [[scholar-luigi-pernier]]) reported finding it. His stated grounds are stylistic and technical: uniquely clean-cut edges, the improbable singularity of a stamped clay document with no comparanda, and the absence of a laboratory authentication.

Protagonist node for the minority position [[claim-phaistos-forgery-hypothesis]], the counterpart to the mainstream defenders.
