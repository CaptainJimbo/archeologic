Population geneticist at Harvard Medical School (David Reich laboratory) specialising in ancient DNA and the peopling of Europe and the Near East. Lead author of the 2017 study on the genetic origins of the Minoans and Mycenaeans ([[source-lazaridis-2017]]), which found broad genetic continuity from the Bronze Age Aegean populations down to modern Greeks. That continuity is evidence against a mass population replacement of the kind a literal Dorian Invasion would require, supporting [[claim-dorian-no-population-signature]].
