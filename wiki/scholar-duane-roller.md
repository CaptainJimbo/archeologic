**Duane W. Roller** is an American classical scholar (Ohio State University) and author of Cleopatra: A Biography (Oxford University Press, 2010), a standard modern scholarly life of Cleopatra VII. His treatment represents the mainstream position reflected in [[claim-cleopatra-greek-ancestry]]: Cleopatra was of Macedonian Greek royal descent and culture, while the identity of her mother, and of Ptolemy XII's mother, is unknown, so a small fraction of her ancestry cannot be pinned down and speculation about substantial Egyptian or African descent is unproven.
