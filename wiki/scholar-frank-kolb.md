Ancient historian (Tübingen) and the most prominent skeptic of the [[scholar-manfred-korfmann|Korfmann]] 'Troia' picture. He argues Late Bronze Age Troy was a minor settlement, not a major trade metropolis, and that the excavation overinterpreted its finds ([[source-kolb-2005]]) — a leading voice against over-historicizing the Trojan War.
