German prehistorian of the German Archaeological Institute (DAI) who recognised the Neolithic megalithic character of [[site-gobekli-tepe]] in 1994 and directed its excavation from 1995 until his death in 2014. His work established the site's Pre-Pottery Neolithic date and made it the central evidence for the argument that monumental sanctuaries were built by pre-agricultural communities.

His 2000 Paleorient preliminary report [[source-schmidt-2000]] is the foundational published account of the 1995-1999 excavations, and he is lead or co-author of the excavation-team syntheses including [[source-dietrich-2012-antiquity]]. He is a key proponent of [[claim-gobekli-tepe-pre-agricultural]].
