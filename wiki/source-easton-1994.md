**Book review (secondary/echo source)** of [[scholar-david-traill]]'s *Excavating Schliemann* (1993), fetched via Cambridge Core. Abstract is unavailable but the references are grounded real text, listing the foundational sceptic literature: Calder III, 'Schliemann on Schliemann: a study in the use of sources' (GRBS 13, 1972) and Calder & Traill, *Myth, Scandal and History* (1986).

Included deliberately to keep [[claim-mask-authenticity-dispute]] **calibrated at contested**, not settled: the review documents that the Schliemann-sceptic case is taken seriously in the discipline, while Easton (a Troy scholar) is among those who hold that Traill and Calder sometimes **overstate** the fabrication charge. Two peer voices, opposite leanings — the reason confidence sits near 0.5.
