**Peer-reviewed article, Science Advances, 2018 (open access).** Verified via the
journal record and PMC (PMC6093623); the paywalled HTML blocked a direct read but
the citation, authorship, and result are confirmed across the indexed record.

Uses annual-resolution radiocarbon measurements on known-age tree rings to refine
the calibration curve around the eruption window. Concludes the data indicate a
**16th-century BCE** date, narrowing — though not eliminating — the offset from
the archaeological chronology. Supports [[claim-thera-high-chronology]];
co-authored by [[scholar-sturt-manning]].

Sits *downstream* of [[source-friedrich-2006]] in the argument but is
methodologically independent (calibration, not the buried branch), so it
genuinely strengthens the high chronology rather than merely echoing it — the
distinction the citogenesis check is built to surface.
