Archaeologist (Duke University) working on the Aegean Bronze Age to Early Iron Age transition, with a regional focus on Crete. In her 2023 Journal of Greek Archaeology study ([[source-judson-2023]]) she examines how the Dorian invasion narrative was built out of early excavation results and why a minimally modified version of it survives despite the failure of a distinct Dorian assemblage to appear, arguing for a complex, regionally diverse transition rather than a single migration. Her work disputes [[claim-dorian-invasion-dark-age]] and supports [[claim-dorian-no-population-signature]].
