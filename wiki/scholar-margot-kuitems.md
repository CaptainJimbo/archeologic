Researcher in radiocarbon dating and dendrochronology at the Centre for Isotope Research, University of Groningen. Lead author of [[source-kuitems-2021]], the 2021 Nature study that used the AD 993 cosmic-ray radiocarbon spike as a tree-ring anchor to date Norse wood at [[site-lanse-aux-meadows]] to exactly AD 1021.

Her work exemplifies single-year 'Miyake event' anchoring, a technique also applied to European dendrochronology (she co-authored related Radiocarbon papers). The 2021 result gives [[claim-lanse-aux-meadows-ad1021]] its precision and is the tightest evidential support for [[claim-norse-precolumbian-america]]. Co-authors include Birgitta Wallace (a Parks Canada authority on the site) and Michael W. Dee (senior author, Groningen).
