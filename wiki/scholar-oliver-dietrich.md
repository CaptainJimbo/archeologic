German prehistorian who worked on the Gobekli Tepe project and has been a leading author of the excavation team's interpretive and chronological publications, continuing the research programme begun by [[scholar-klaus-schmidt]] at [[site-gobekli-tepe]].

He is lead author of the 2012 Antiquity synthesis on cult and feasting [[source-dietrich-2012-antiquity]] and a co-author of the Encyclopedia of Global Archaeology review on agriculture and domestication at the site [[source-peters-2020-domestication]]. His publications support and refine [[claim-gobekli-tepe-pre-agricultural]], including work on the imagery, radiocarbon dating, and the debate over incipient plant use.
