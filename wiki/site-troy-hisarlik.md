The multi-period settlement mound of Hisarlik in the Troad (NW Türkiye), excavated by [[scholar-heinrich-schliemann|Schliemann]] from the 1870s, then Carl Blegen, then [[scholar-manfred-korfmann|Korfmann]]. Identified with Homer's Troy/Ilios. Occupied across the Bronze Age (Troy I-VII); the Late Bronze Age phases Troy VI and VIIa are the candidates for 'Homer's Troy' and the setting of [[claim-troy-vi-viia-war-destruction|its destruction]].
