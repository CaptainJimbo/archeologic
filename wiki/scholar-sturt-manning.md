Archaeologist and dendrochronologist (Cornell University), a leading voice for the
radiocarbon-based [[claim-thera-high-chronology|high chronology]] of the Thera
eruption and a prolific writer on the Aegean–Egyptian dating conflict. Co-author
of [[source-pearson-2018]].

Included as the Thera cluster's scholar node — the counterpart to
[[scholar-antonis-bartsiokas]] in the Vergina cluster — so the graph shows that
each debate has identifiable protagonists, not just disembodied "evidence."
