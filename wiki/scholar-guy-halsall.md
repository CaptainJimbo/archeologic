Historian of the early medieval West (University of York), author of Worlds of Arthur: Facts and Fictions of the Dark Ages (Oxford, 2014). A leading skeptic of Arthurian historicity: he argues the literary sources cannot establish whether Arthur existed and that the sensible response is to ask the right questions of the evidence rather than force answers from it. His position is summarised in the open-access review [[source-lazarescu-2015]].
