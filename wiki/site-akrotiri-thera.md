The Late Bronze Age Cycladic town on the island of Thera (Santorini), buried and
preserved under metres of volcanic tephra by the eruption — the "Aegean Pompeii,"
with multi-storey houses and wall frescoes.

Akrotiri is the geographic anchor of the Thera cluster. The eruption that sealed
it is what both [[claim-thera-high-chronology]] and [[claim-thera-low-chronology]]
are trying to date. A place node, not a document.
