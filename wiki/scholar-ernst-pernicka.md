German-Austrian archaeometallurgist and analytical chemist, a leading authority on the material analysis of the Nebra Sky Disc and long the scientific director of the Curt-Engelhorn-Zentrum Archaeometrie in Mannheim. He led or co-authored the core provenance and composition studies underpinning the Early Bronze Age attribution, including the Cornish-gold provenance work [[source-ehser-2011]], and is lead author of the 2020 interdisciplinary rebuttal [[source-pernicka-2020]] to the Iron-Age reattribution. His work is the empirical backbone of the mainstream position on [[claim-nebra-disc-bronze-age]] and the principal counter to [[claim-nebra-iron-age-challenge]].
