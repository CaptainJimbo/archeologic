Rupert Gebhard (director of the Archaeological State Collection, Munich) and Ruediger Krause (professor of prehistory, Goethe University Frankfurt), joint authors of the 2020 article that questioned the Nebra Sky Disc's Early Bronze Age date and its association with the Mittelberg hoard. They argued the disc should be typologically placed in the Iron Age and that it may not have been found with the hoard or even on the Mittelberg, treating the looter-derived context as unreliable. Their proposal is the basis of [[claim-nebra-iron-age-challenge]]. It was rapidly and forcefully rebutted by [[scholar-ernst-pernicka]] and colleagues in [[source-pernicka-2020]]; the Early Bronze Age attribution [[claim-nebra-disc-bronze-age]] remains the scholarly consensus.
